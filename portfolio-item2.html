Black Jack

A Simple Black Jack Game

The Novice

This simple Blackjack game brings the classic casino experience to your screen. The goal is to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. Players receive two cards to start and can "hit" to add more cards or "stand" to keep their hand. The dealer will reveal their cards and must hit until reaching at least 17.

If the player's hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without exceeding it, they win! With a straightforward design, quick gameplay, and clear rules, this version of Blackjack is perfect for both beginners and seasoned players looking to enjoy a fun and easy card game experience.
